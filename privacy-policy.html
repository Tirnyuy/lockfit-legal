Privacy Policy – LockFit ⸻ Last updated: November 2025 This Privacy Policy explains how LockFit (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the LockFit mobile application (the “App”). By using LockFit, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. ⸻ 1. Information We Collect We aim to collect only the minimum data necessary to provide and improve the App. 1.1. Information you provide to us • Account-related information (if any): If the app allows you to create an account or save preferences, we may collect information such as your name or email address. • Feedback and support: If you contact us for support or send feedback (for example through in-app forms or a feedback tool), we collect the information you choose to share. 1.2. Automatically collected information When you use LockFit, we may automatically collect: • App usage data: Basic information about how you interact with the App’s features (for example, which screens are viewed, session length, buttons tapped). • Device information: Such as device model, operating system version, app version, language, and country. • Notification and reminder settings: For example, your chosen exercise reminders or challenge schedules. We do not sell your personal data. ⸻ 3. Subscriptions & Payments LockFit may offer premium features through in-app purchases or subscriptions. • Purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple’s App Store billing system. • We do not receive or store your full payment card information. • We may receive limited information from Apple (such as transaction identifiers or subscription status) in order to unlock premium content and manage your access. You can read more about RevenueCat’s privacy practices in their own Privacy Policy. ⸻ 4.
