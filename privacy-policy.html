Privacy Policy – LockFit

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how LockFit (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the LockFit mobile application (the “App”).

By using LockFit, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We aim to collect only the minimum data necessary to provide and improve the App.

1.1 Information you provide to us

  • Account-related information (if applicable): If the app allows you to create an account or save preferences, we may collect information such as your name or email address.
  • Feedback and support: If you contact us for support or send feedback, we collect the information you choose to share.

1.2 Automatically collected information

When you use LockFit, we may automatically collect:

  • App usage data (screens visited, feature interactions, time spent)
  • Device information (model, OS version, app version, language)
  • Notification and reminder settings

1.3 Camera Usage

Some features use your camera for exercise tracking or posture detection. We do not store or transmit any camera images or video. All processing is done locally on your device or via approved SDKs according to their own privacy policies.

1.4 Notifications

LockFit uses local notifications for reminders about exercises, challenges, and streaks. You can turn off notifications anytime in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and operate app features
  • Maintain streaks, challenge progress, and statistics
  • Improve performance, stability, and user experience
  • Detect misuse and ensure app security
  • Communicate updates or important notices

3. Subscriptions & Payments

LockFit offers optional premium features through in-app purchases. All payments are processed securely by Apple’s App Store. We never see or store your credit card information.

We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status and entitlements. RevenueCat handles:

  • Validating your App Store purchases
  • Determining if you should have Premium access
  • Restoring past purchases

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to improve functionality:

  • Firebase (Google): analytics, crash reporting, stability improvements
  • RevenueCat: subscription and purchase management
  • Feedback tools: allowing users to send feature requests or bug reports

These services may process anonymized usage data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to operate the App, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights such as:

  • Requesting access to your data
  • Requesting deletion of your data
  • Objecting to data processing
  • Withdrawing consent (where applicable)

To exercise your rights, contact us using the email below.

8. Children’s Privacy

LockFit is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. International Transfers

Some third-party providers may process data in other countries. We ensure these transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.
