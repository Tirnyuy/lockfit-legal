Privacy Policy – LockFit

Last updated: November 2025

1. Information We Collect

We aim to collect only the minimum data necessary to provide and improve the App.

1.1 Information you provide to us

  • Account-related information (if any): If the app allows you to create an account or save preferences, we may collect information such as your name or email address.
  • Feedback and support: If you contact us for support or send feedback, we collect the information you choose to share.

1.2 Automatically collected information

When you use LockFit, we may automatically collect:

  • App usage data (which screens you visit, buttons tapped, etc.)
  • Device information (model, OS version, app version)
  • Notification and reminder settings

1.3 Camera Usage

Some features use your camera for exercise tracking. We do not store or transmit camera data.

1.4 Notifications

LockFit uses local notifications for reminders. You can disable them anytime in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate app features
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Maintain challenge progress, streaks, and stats
  • Prevent abuse and ensure app security

3. Subscriptions & Payments

Subscriptions are handled by Apple’s App Store. We do not store your payment information. RevenueCat processes subscription data for entitlement validation.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase – analytics and crash reporting
  • RevenueCat – subscription management
  • Feedback tools – optional user feedback collection

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your data.

You may request deletion or access to your data by contacting us.
